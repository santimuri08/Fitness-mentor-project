COMMON QUESTIONS

How much time do I need to commit to workouts?

+

My programs are designed to fit your schedule. For optimal results, I recommend 3-4 workouts per week, each lasting 45-60 minutes. However, I can create effective programs with as little as 30 minutes, 3 times per week for those with very busy schedules.

What equipment do I need?

+

Programs can be tailored to any equipment situation. Home-based programs typically work best with at least a set of adjustable dumbbells and a bench. Gym-based programs utilize full equipment access. During our consultation, we'll discuss your available equipment and design a program accordingly.

Do you offer nutrition plans?

+

Yes, the 1:1 Online Coaching includes personalized nutrition guidance based on your goals, food preferences, and lifestyle. Rather than rigid meal plans, I provide customized calorie and macro targets, food recommendations, and meal timing strategies to ensure sustainable results.

How is the AI used in your coaching?

+

The AI component of my coaching creates personalized motivational content, workout reminders, and educational material to complement our human coaching relationship. The AI helps maintain daily motivation and accountability, while I provide the expertise, program design, and personalized feedback that only a human coach can deliver.

How long before I see results?

+

Most clients notice improved energy and workout performance within 2 weeks. Visible physical changes typically begin around 4-6 weeks of consistent effort. Significant transformations are usually achieved within 3-6 months, depending on your starting point and goals. Remember, the most sustainable results come from consistent, long-term habits.

Can I cancel my subscription?

+

Yes, you can cancel at any time with no long-term commitment required. I believe in earning your business every month through results and service quality. For best results, however, I recommend committing to at least 3 months to give your body time to adapt and change.
