FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What happens during the consultation call?

+

During our 15-minute call, we'll discuss your fitness goals, current routine, challenges you're facing, and how my coaching approach might help. This is a no-pressure conversation to determine if we're a good fit to work together.

Do I need to prepare anything for the call?

+

It helps to have a clear idea of your fitness goals, time availability for workouts, any equipment you have access to, and specific challenges you're facing.This allows me to provide more targeted advice during our call.

What if I need to reschedule my call?

+

No problem! You can easily reschedule through the confirmation email you'll receive after booking. I understand schedules change, and I'm flexible to accommodate.

How much does coaching cost?

+

Coaching packages start at $99/month for customized workout plans and $199/month for comprehensive plans with nutrition guidance and weekly check-ins. During our call, we can discuss which option best fits your needs and budget.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

+

Yes, I offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your coaching experience within the first two weeks, I'll provide a full refund. Your success and satisfaction are my top priorities.
